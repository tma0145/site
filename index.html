Synopsis

Gris dives into the journey of a young girl who deals with loss, and her journey to move past the loss. The journey is

visually depicted by the color being drained from her dress in the beginning, and as she moves through the different levels and world, gains more and more


Visual Aesthetics




As the game progress and as the playable character goes through the world and completes the puzzles, she gains color at each level she goes through

She starts out as black and white, then she gains red, green, blue, and then at the end, she reunites with the broken statue as a whole again,which had broken apart at the beginning of the game, causing her to lose her voice.
